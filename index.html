📖 How to Use
1
Copy & Paste Copy any reel/video link, paste it into the input box, add an optional note, and tap Save.
2
Install as App (PWA) In your browser tap the menu → "Add to Home Screen". This installs ReelsVault as a real app.
3
Share from Instagram/TikTok Open a reel → tap Share (paper-plane icon) → "More" → select ReelsVault from the app list. The link auto-fills!
4
Organise & Search Filter by platform, search notes, mark favourites, or copy/open any saved link anytime.
5
Export / Backup Tap Export on the Settings tab to download all your links as a JSON file.
